ingridents needed to bake a cake

1/2 cup of sugar 2 eggs 50 grams of flour vanilla esscence 2 table spoon white choclate 10 grams

how to bake a cake:

  1. seprate the yolks from the eggs
  2. mix the yolks with the flour
  3. add vanilla esscence to the mixture
  4. bake the miture at 120 degree celcius for 70 minutes
  5. add white choclate as frosting
